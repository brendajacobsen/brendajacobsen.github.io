Site Purpose

My Weather Buddy is not your average weather website. My Weather Budddy will be there for you not only to tell you the weather ahead of time but also to advice you with the apropiate clothing to wear according to the weather during the time frame you select. It could be according to today's weather or a week's worth of weather. This is the perfect site for either the regular person that needs assistence on what to wear for the day or the traveler who needs to pack a week worth of outfits. Personalized to your needs, activities and location.
