Site Name

My Weather Stylist

Site Purpose

My Weather Stylist is not your average weather website. My Weather Stylist will be there for you not only to tell you the weather ahead of time but also to advise you on the appropriate clothing to wear according to the weather during the time frame you select. It could be for today's weather or up to a week's worth of weather in advance. My Weather Stylist can be personalized to your needs, activities and location. This is the perfect site for either the regular person that needs assistance on what to wear for the day or the traveler who needs to pack a week worth of outfits for a trip.
