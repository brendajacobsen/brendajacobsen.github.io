Target Audience

The target audience for My Weather Stylist are male and female, single or in a relationship between the age of 25 to 45. They have a pretty stable financial situation with a mid level to mid-high income level. They have busy schedules that keep them busy during the day going to different places, attending their day time jobs, meetings, events, etc. They live in busy cities and are more likely to use public transportation for most of their commutes. The target audience also likes to travel a lot or thay have to due to work related reasons. They are organized people that like to follow an agenda and improvissing is hardly ever an option. This audience also takes care of its apperance or follow a dress code and they also carry their cellphones with them everywhere they go as well as a laptop.

Persona

Name: Martha

Occupation:Journalist

Age: 29 years old

General Information: A single hard working journalist in the city of New York that takes the subway to get to work and to move around the city on her days off. Walking to places is also a big part of her days.

Goals: Martha likes to plan her outfits one day ahead and make sure that she is going to dress weather appropiate for the entire day.

Name: Hector

Occupation: Blogger

Age: 32 years old

General Information: Hector has made a living from his photography talent and the pictures he shares from his many trips around the world.

Goals:Needs assistance when packing for his trips. He travels to all kinds of destinations where weather sometimes varies drastically.

Scenarios

  • What should I wear if I'm going to be out all day today?
