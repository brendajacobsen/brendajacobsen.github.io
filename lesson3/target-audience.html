Target Audience

The target audience for My Weather Stylist are male and female between the age of 25 to 45. They have a pretty stable financial situation with a medium to high income. They have busy schedules that keep them busy during the day going to different places, attending their day time jobs, meetings, events, etc. The target audience also likes to travel a lot or thay have to due to work related reasons. They are organized people that like to follow an agenda and improvissing is hardly ever an option. This audience also takes care of its apperance or follow a dress code and they also carry their cellphones with them everywhere they go as well as a laptop.
